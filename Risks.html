the risk of Artificial Intelligence page

    There is no place for emotions: all human perceptions of conscience, mercy and collective spirit will be removed,i.e. tools that can lead to many tasks without there being connections with humans, that artificial intelligence cannot replace human relationships. Unemployment: A person cannot match the machine in terms of efficiency by achieving machines in the place of people in various practical field, which increases the unemployment rate. High cost: working in this field requires a lot of money both in machinery manufacturing operations, and in programming them for repair.Data loss: Despite the enormous memory that may be processed with these machines but there will be no link between this information when storing and retrieving it as humans do, and some damage to the machine can lead to the loss of all data stored on it.
